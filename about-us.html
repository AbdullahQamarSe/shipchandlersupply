About Us

Supply Ship Services Pte Ltd, based in Singapore, is a global provider of food provisions for vessels at various ports worldwide. Our mission is to ensure that every ship is well-stocked with the highest quality supplies, maintaining operational efficiency and crew well-being. With a commitment to reliability and excellence, we serve a diverse range of clients, delivering customized solutions to meet the unique needs of each vessel. Trust us to be your dependable partner in maritime provisioning.
